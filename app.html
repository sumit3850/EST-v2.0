About EST
Entomological Surveillance Tool
v.2.0.60 · Online / Offline · Mobile-First
About the Application
Entomological Surveillance Tool (EST) is a comprehensive, online / offline, mobile-first digital platform for systematic mosquito vector surveillance in public health and vector control operations.

Purpose-built for field entomologists, public health workers, and vector control personnel, EST enables structured data collection, real-time epidemiological index computation, GPS-tagged site mapping, and professional report generation — entirely without requiring continuous internet connectivity.

Built as a Progressive Web App (PWA), it operates seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. All survey data is stored locally on the device and can be synced to a GitHub-backed cloud database whenever connectivity is available.
Surveillance Modules
1. Adult Mosquito Surveillance — 4 Sub-Modules
• Adult Mosquito Density — Resting Collection (Indoor/Outdoor Aspirator)
• Human Landing Catch (HLC) — Per-site human-bait landing collection
• Human Biting Rate (HBR) — Overnight volunteer bait collection
• Adult Biting / Landing Rate (ALR) — Trap or station-based collection
• 25 species across 5 genera; UN-ID log for unidentified specimens
• Per-house GPS auto-tagged; live survey path on satellite map

2. Larval / Immature Stage Surveillance
• Aedes — 18 container-type matrix; Stegomyia Indices (HI, CI, BI, Pupal Index)
• Anopheles / Culex — Independent dip-site logging (DS-001…); LD, PD, IMD (/100 dips)
• Genus-level identification: Aedes sp., Culex sp., Anopheles sp. + UN-ID log
• Per-house & per-site GPS auto-tagged; positive houses on satellite map
• KML export for Google Earth / Google Maps
WHO-Standard Indices
Adult Mosquito Density (Resting): PMHD, TMHD (×10), Indoor/Outdoor Resting Density, Room Density
Adult Mosquito Density (HLC): PMHD, TMHD, HBR, HBR/hr
Human Biting Rate (HBR): HBR (mosq/night/volunteer), HBR/hr, PMHD, TMHD
Adult Biting/Landing Rate (ALR): ALR (adults/bait/hr), ALR/night, ALR Simple
Larval (Aedes): HI, CI, BI, Pupal Index
Larval (An./Cx.): Larval Density, Pupal Density, Immature Density

PMHD Formula: Total Mosquitoes ÷ (Collectors × Total Time hrs)
TMHD Formula: (Culex ÷ (Collectors × Total Time hrs)) × 10
HBR Formula: Total Mosquitoes ÷ Nights ÷ Volunteers
ALR Formula: Total Mosquitoes ÷ (Bait Stations × Hours)
Key Advantages
• Fully offline — single HTML file, any device
• GPS auto-detect with 4-API network fallback
• Survey path recording — live GPS track with distance & time timer
• Per-house GPS logging — each house auto-captures GPS position on log
• Satellite map preview — survey path + positive houses on Google Satellite
• All India states/UTs and districts
• Draft auto-save — never lose in-progress data
• Export — CSV/Excel with GPS + Google Maps links, KML for Google Earth, PDF, WhatsApp, Email
• Data persists on reload (localStorage)
• Two modules — Adult & Larval in one app
