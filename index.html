About EST

About the Application
EST v2.0 is a comprehensive, fully offline mobile-first tool for entomological surveillance of mosquito vectors in the field. It combines Adult Mosquito Collection and Larval/Immature Stage Surveillance into a single app — designed for public health workers, field entomologists, and vector control staff across India.

All data is stored locally on the device and persists across sessions. No internet required after download.
Surveillance Modules
1. Adult Mosquito Surveillance
• Resting Collection — Indoor/Outdoor counts
• Human Landing Catch (HLC) — Per-site collection
• 27 species across 5 genera
• Per-house time-stamped entries with GPS

2. Larval Surveillance
• Aedes — 18 container types, stegomyia indices
• Anopheles/Culex — dipping data, density indices
• 8 larval species identification
• Per-house entries with house-positive detection
WHO-Standard Indices
Adult (Resting): PMHD, TMHD (×10), Indoor/Outdoor Resting Density, Room Density
Adult (HLC): HBR, HBR/hr, PMHD, TMHD
Larval (Aedes): HI, CI, BI, Pupal Index
Larval (An./Cx.): Larval Density, Pupal Density, Immature Density

PMHD Formula: (Non-Culex ÷ Collectors × Time hrs) × 100
TMHD Formula: (Culex ÷ Man-hours) × 10
Key Advantages
• Fully offline — single HTML file, any device
• GPS auto-detect with 4-API network fallback
• All India states/UTs and districts
• Draft auto-save — never lose in-progress data
• Export — CSV, PDF report, WhatsApp, Email
• Data persists on reload (localStorage)
• Two modules — Adult & Larval in one app
