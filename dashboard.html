EST Database

The command centre of the Entomological Surveillance Tool — a cloud-synced platform for WHO-standard mosquito surveillance across the Andaman & Nicobar Islands. Field teams collect surveys offline on their phones; everything lands here, analysed and export-ready.

Live survey database

Every GPS-tracked and manual survey from the field, synced into one central record.

Analytics & risk maps

District and zone HI · CI · BI indices, breeding hotspots and satellite survey maps.

Reports & exports

Monthly NVBDCP formats, entomological risk reports, and bulk PDF / Excel / CSV export.

Team & access control

Approve field workers, manage credentials, and keep survey access admin-controlled.
